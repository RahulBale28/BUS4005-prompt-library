# Staff Incident Report Narrative
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** Clinical Governance — Triggered when a staff member initiates an incident report in the quality management system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Writing incident report narratives takes 20–30 minutes per event due to uncertainty about structure and language. This contributes to under-reporting of near-miss events, which carries significant patient safety risk.

**Automation Potential:** Medium — AI generates draft narrative from structured inputs. Clinical governance officer reviews before submission. Expected to increase near-miss reporting by 25–35%.

**Key Risks:** AI must not include liability language; staff may give minimal inputs; reports may involve staff distress requiring sensitive handling.

---

### Prompt Text

Write an incident report about a workplace event.
