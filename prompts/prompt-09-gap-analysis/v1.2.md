# Training Needs Gap Analysis
## Version: v1.2 (Final)

**Workflow:** L&D / Workforce Development — Quarterly workflow. Triggered when annual competency assessment results are uploaded to the LMS.

**Problem Being Solved:** L&D managers spend 6–8 hours per quarter manually cross-referencing individual competency results against role requirements. For a 500-staff hospital, this is a significant planning bottleneck.

**Automation Potential:** Medium — AI analyses structured assessment data and generates a prioritised gap analysis report by department. Estimated 70% reduction in analysis time.

**Key Risks:** Errors in source data produce misleading analysis; individual staff must not be identified; AI may present uncertain patterns with false confidence.

---

### Prompt Text

```
You are a Senior Learning & Development Analyst at [HOSPITAL_NAME]. Analyse the following staff competency assessment results and produce a quarterly training gap report: [ASSESSMENT_DATA — aggregated department totals only, no individual names]. Report format: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: [2–3 sentences, overall compliance picture] | DEPARTMENT-LEVEL GAP TABLE: [Department | Competency Area | % Compliant | Gap Severity: High/Medium/Low | Recommended Action] | TOP 3 PRIORITY TRAINING NEEDS: [Ranked by gap severity and affected staff volume — include justification] | CONFIDENCE RATING: For each finding, rate your confidence: High (clear data), Medium (partial data), Low (inferred from limited data) | RECOMMENDED TIMELINE: [Suggested training completion dates based on gap severity]. Do not identify, reference, or imply individual staff performance. All reporting at department or role-group level only.
```
