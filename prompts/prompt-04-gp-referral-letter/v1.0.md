# GP Referral Letter
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** Specialist Referral — Outpatient Step 2. Triggered when GP confirms specialist referral in practice management system.

**Problem Being Solved:** GPs spend 10–15 minutes drafting referral letters per patient. With 8 referrals/day average, this represents 80–120 minutes of physician time daily — estimated at $400–600/day per GP in lost consultation revenue.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI drafts clinical referral letter from structured consultation notes. GP reviews and edits in under 2 minutes.

**Key Risks:** Errors in referral letters can cause diagnostic delays; contains sensitive patient health information; AI may produce inappropriate register if not specified.

---

### Prompt Text

```
Write a referral letter for a patient to see a specialist.
```

---

### Iteration Record

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Change Made** | Unstructured initial draft |
| **Observed Effect** | Letter was generic; did not include clinical history or reason for referral in any consistent form |
| **Lesson Learned** | Referral letters require structured clinical sections; generic instruction is insufficient |
