# Clinical Handover Summary
## Version: v1.1

**Workflow:** Nursing Handover — End-of-shift documentation. Triggered when outgoing nurse completes digital patient chart update.

**Problem Being Solved:** Nurses spend 15–20 minutes per patient writing shift handover notes, causing delays and inconsistent documentation. Poor handovers are linked to 24% of adverse clinical events (ACSQHC, 2017).

**Automation Potential:** Medium-High — AI drafts ISBAR-format handover from chart data. Nurse reviews and approves in under 3 minutes. Potential 60% reduction in documentation time.

**Key Risks:** AI may omit critical flags if chart data is incomplete; AI-generated handovers cannot replace clinical judgement; AI may underweight atypical symptoms.

---

### Prompt Text

You are a clinical nurse. Write a shift handover summary for patient [PATIENT_ID] using the ISBAR framework: Identity, Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation. Use data provided: [DATA_BLOCK].
