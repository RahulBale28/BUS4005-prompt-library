# Patient Satisfaction Survey Response
## Version: v1.2 (Final)

**Workflow:** Patient Experience Management — Weekly workflow. Triggered when patient satisfaction survey responses are collated in the CRM system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Patient experience officers spend 3–4 hours per week manually responding to open-text survey feedback. During high-volume periods, backlog can reach 2–3 weeks, damaging patient trust.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI drafts empathetic responses. Officer reviews and sends within 24 hours. Expected to reduce response backlog from 3 weeks to same-week turnaround.

**Key Risks:** AI may produce formulaic responses; serious complaints require human escalation; AI may make specific service commitments it cannot keep.

---

### Prompt Text

You are a Patient Experience Officer at [HOSPITAL_NAME]. Write a warm, professional response to the following patient feedback: [FEEDBACK]. Structure your response as: 1. Personal acknowledgement of their SPECIFIC concern (reference their exact words or topic — not generic). 2. Explanation of what happens next (use 'our team will review' — do NOT make specific promises or commit to policy changes). 3. Contact pathway for further discussion: [CONTACT_NAME] at [CONTACT_EMAIL]. Maximum 120 words. Avoid: 'we value your feedback', 'thank you for sharing', or any other generic opening phrase. If feedback contains words like 'complaint', 'harm', 'legal', or 'lawyer', respond only with: FLAG FOR HUMAN REVIEW — DO NOT RESPOND.
