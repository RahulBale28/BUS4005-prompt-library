# Patient Satisfaction Survey Response
## Version: v1.1

**Workflow:** Patient Experience Management — Weekly workflow. Triggered when patient satisfaction survey responses are collated in the CRM system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Patient experience officers spend 3–4 hours per week manually responding to open-text survey feedback. During high-volume periods, backlog can reach 2–3 weeks, damaging patient trust.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI drafts empathetic responses. Officer reviews and sends within 24 hours. Expected to reduce response backlog from 3 weeks to same-week turnaround.

**Key Risks:** AI may produce formulaic responses; serious complaints require human escalation; AI may make specific service commitments it cannot keep.

---

### Prompt Text

You are a patient experience officer. Write an empathetic, professional reply to this patient survey feedback: [FEEDBACK]. Acknowledge their specific concern. Keep under 150 words.
