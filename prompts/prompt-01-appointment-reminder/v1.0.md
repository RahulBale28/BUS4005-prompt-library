# Patient Appointment Reminder
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** Outpatient Scheduling — Step 1 of patient communication chain. Triggered automatically 48 hours before appointment via EMR integration.

**Problem Being Solved:** Administrative staff spend approximately 30 minutes per day manually calling patients for reminder calls. With 80+ daily appointments, this consumes ~4 hours of clinical admin time and still results in a 14% no-show rate.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI generates personalised reminder messages for each patient. Staff review batch of 20 messages in under 5 minutes before SMS send. Potential to reduce no-show rate by 30–40%.

**Key Risks:** Hallucination risk if EMR data contains errors; privacy risk from including clinical data; over-reliance risk if staff batch-approve without reviewing.

---

### Prompt Text

```
Write a reminder message for a patient about their appointment.
```
