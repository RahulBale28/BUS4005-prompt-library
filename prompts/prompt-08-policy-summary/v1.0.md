# Internal Policy Summary for Staff
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** Policy & Compliance — Triggered when a new or updated policy is published in the document management system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Full healthcare policies are typically 15–40 pages. Communications officers spend 2–3 hours per policy converting documents into staff-readable summaries, often delaying compliance implementation.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI reads policy document text and generates a structured staff summary. Communications officer reviews in 15 minutes. Turnaround reduced from 1–2 weeks to 48 hours.

**Key Risks:** AI may miss critical compliance obligations; simplifying legal language may alter meaning; policy versions must be tracked accurately.

---

### Prompt Text

Summarise this policy for staff: [POLICY_TEXT].

---

### Iteration Record

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Change Made** | No structure, no audience specification |
| **Observed Effect** | Output was a shorter version of the document — not a staff-friendly summary; technical language retained |
| **Lesson Learned** | Summarisation without audience and format constraints reproduces source language at reduced length |
