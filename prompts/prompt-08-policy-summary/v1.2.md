# Internal Policy Summary for Staff
## Version: v1.2 (Final)

**Workflow:** Policy & Compliance — Triggered when a new or updated policy is published in the document management system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Full healthcare policies are typically 15–40 pages. Communications officers spend 2–3 hours per policy converting documents into staff-readable summaries, often delaying compliance implementation.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI reads policy document text and generates a structured staff summary. Communications officer reviews in 15 minutes. Turnaround reduced from 1–2 weeks to 48 hours.

**Key Risks:** AI may miss critical compliance obligations; simplifying legal language may alter meaning; policy versions must be tracked accurately.

---

### Prompt Text

You are an Internal Communications Officer at [HOSPITAL_NAME]. Convert the following policy document into a clear staff bulletin: [POLICY_TEXT]. Use this format: POLICY ALERT — [POLICY_NAME] | Version: [VERSION_NUMBER] | Effective: [EFFECTIVE_DATE] | Approved by: [APPROVER] | WHAT HAS CHANGED: [2–3 bullet points — what is new or different from previous version] | WHAT YOU MUST DO: [Numbered list of specific, actionable staff obligations — 3–6 items] | KEY DATES TO NOTE: [Any compliance deadlines] | LEGISLATIVE ITEMS: [Any obligations arising from legislation — quote VERBATIM, do not paraphrase, label each as 'Direct Legal Requirement'] | QUESTIONS? Contact: [POLICY_CONTACT]. Maximum 400 words. Write at Year 9 reading level.
