# Job Posting — Clinical Roles
## Version: v1.2 (Final)

**Workflow:** HR Recruitment — Step 1 of hiring workflow. Triggered when department head approves a vacancy in the HR system.

**Problem Being Solved:** HR generalists spend 45–60 minutes per vacancy drafting clinical job advertisements. At 80+ hires/year, this represents 60–80 hours of HR time annually.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI generates role-specific, compliant job ad from structured role brief. HR reviews and publishes in under 10 minutes.

**Key Risks:** AI may reproduce gendered language; clinical roles have mandatory AHPRA registration requirements; AI may not reflect hospital brand voice.

---

### Prompt Text

```
You are an experienced Healthcare HR Officer at [HOSPITAL_NAME]. Write a compelling, inclusive job advertisement for a [ROLE_TITLE] role in the [DEPARTMENT] department. Use this structure: ROLE TITLE & CLASSIFICATION | ABOUT US: [2 sentences using brand language: caring, community-centred, evidence-based] | ABOUT THE ROLE: [4–5 key responsibilities — action verbs, specific not generic] | WHAT YOU BRING: [Required: AHPRA registration as [REGISTRATION_TYPE], [QUALIFICATION], [EXPERIENCE_YEARS] years experience. Desirable: [DESIRABLE_SKILLS]] | WHAT WE OFFER: [Salary band: [SALARY_BAND] per [AWARD], flexible rostering, professional development] | INCLUSION STATEMENT: [1 sentence welcoming applications from all backgrounds]. Use gender-neutral language throughout. Avoid: 'passionate', 'rockstar', 'dynamic'. Maximum 350 words.
```

### Iteration Record

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Change Made** | Added full structure, AHPRA registration type variable, salary band requirement, prohibited words list, word limit |
