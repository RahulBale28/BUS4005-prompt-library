# Job Posting — Clinical Roles
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** HR Recruitment — Step 1 of hiring workflow. Triggered when department head approves a vacancy in the HR system.

**Problem Being Solved:** HR generalists spend 45–60 minutes per vacancy drafting clinical job advertisements. At 80+ hires/year, this represents 60–80 hours of HR time annually.

**Automation Potential:** High — AI generates role-specific, compliant job ad from structured role brief. HR reviews and publishes in under 10 minutes.

**Key Risks:** AI may reproduce gendered language; clinical roles have mandatory AHPRA registration requirements; AI may not reflect hospital brand voice.

---

### Prompt Text

Write a job ad for a nurse at a hospital.
