# Medication Discharge Instructions
## Version: v1.0

**Workflow:** Discharge Planning — Step 3 of patient discharge workflow. Triggered after pharmacist confirms medication list in dispensing system.

**Problem Being Solved:** Pharmacy and clinical staff spend 25–45 minutes per patient producing plain-language medication instructions. Readmission rates linked to medication misunderstanding are estimated at 20% (AIHW, 2022).

**Automation Potential:** High — AI translates clinical medication data into plain-language instructions. Physician reviews in under 2 minutes. Scalable across all discharge patients.

**Key Risks:** Incorrect dosing instructions could harm patients; AI may still use clinical language; non-English-speaking patients not served by AI translation.

---

### Prompt Text

```
Write medication instructions for a patient being discharged.
```
